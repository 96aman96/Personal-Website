Project Outline

Farm Chores

Overview:

I am made farm chores because I wanted to practise my hand at mini games. Farm chores at its time of release had 10 mini games in it. The mini games are all related to every day farm life and include doing things like driving a tractor to harvest crops, sorting fruits and vegetables using your axe to chop plumber, using the chainsaw to knock down trees and even harvesting your pumpkin yield.

Roles:

I wrote the code for all ten minigames and with art used from the Unity Asset Store. Farm chores was made in my early days of learning Unity so all art belong to the asset store.

I also integrated multiple analytics and ad network SDKs including but not limited to

  • Voodoo Analytics
  • Facebook Analytics
  • GameAnalytics
  • MoPub
  • AdMob
  • Google Ads

Learnings:

While I have always been good at writing one concrete game, it was a great learning experience when I got to write multiple mini games all wrapped in one project. Handling data across different mini games, implementing novel controls and more specifically testing each mini game until it felt right was very insightful. This project also helped me familiarise myself with all the possibilities of user input available on mobile devices.
