2. What (purposes) do I use the data for

3. What is my legal basis for the processing

For both analytics and advertising I base the processing of personal data on the legitimate goal of improving my games. For analytics, this is my ability to analyze how players progress through my games to make them better, and to know my player-base on an aggregate level. For advertising, this is my ability to be able to off-set development costs and risk with advertizing income.

For the third-parties I use, their legitimate interest is to be able to offer me their services, and for advertising third-parties and advertisers to be able to show you advertising.
