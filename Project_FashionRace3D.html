Project Outline

Fashion Race 3D

Overview:

Fashion Race 3D is a game that was originally inspired by another hit hyper casual game called Bridge Race by one of my clients called Supersonic Studios. The player plays as one of the four women in a swimsuit and across segments of the level their job is to collect stacks of money in the same colour as their outfit. The player must deposit the collected money at the showcase and once enough money has been deposited a part of the outfit is made available to the player. The character who collects all three parts of an outfit before the others wins the level. Enemies can bump into you and you can bump into them thereby making the character drop money which can later be stolen by enemies this also allows you to steal money from opponents.

Roles:

I was responsible for writing the code for the entire game including the code for movement, bot character set up, money spawning and other controls. The models for money were made by me along with the all UI.
